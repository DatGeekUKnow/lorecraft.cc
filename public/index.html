Come Join Us!

LoreCraft opened on January 2, 2022, with the merger of two Minecraft servers from Harding University and Lipscomb University. We are primarily a Christian community with shared roots in the churches of Christ, and we welcome Christians of all denominations and non-Christians alike with open arms.Our server has a long history of worldbuilding. As such, players are encouraged to build nations, governments, religions, companies, cities, economies, and anything else to contribute to our shared history and lore.
